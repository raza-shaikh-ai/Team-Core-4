How It Works

Three simple steps to turn surplus into sustenance

01
👨‍🌾

Farmer Lists Surplus

Farmers log their excess harvest — crop type, quantity, harvest date, and pickup location — in just seconds.

02
🔍

NGOs Browse & Request

Nearby NGOs and food banks discover available produce on an interactive map, filter by location, and submit pickup requests.

03
🚚

Pickup & Deliver

Farmers approve requests, schedule pickups, and fresh produce reaches communities that need it most — zero waste.

About FarmShare

Every year, millions of tonnes of perfectly edible farm produce go to waste — while communities go hungry. FarmShare was built to change that story.

We connect farmers who have surplus harvest directly with NGOs and food banks in their area. No middlemen, no red tape — just fresh produce reaching the people who need it most.

Our mission is simple: zero food waste, zero hunger. Join us in building a more sustainable and compassionate food system.
